Strategy reminder: Before solving, ask yourself — is a variable already isolated? (→ Substitution). Are both equations in Ax + By = C form? (→ Elimination). Does the question ask for x + y or a combination? (→ SAT Shortcut). Does the problem involve finding k for no/infinite solutions? (→ Algebra, compare slopes).
